How to use Pen Collection

What is this?

Pen Collection is a personal inventory app for tracking your pen collection. Everything is stored privately in your browser — no account, no server, no internet connection needed after the page loads.

Adding a pen

Click + Add Pen in the top right. You can upload up to 3 photos per pen — the first photo becomes the cover image shown in the grid. Then fill in as many or as few fields as you like:

  • Brand and model
  • Pen type, refill / nib type, tip size and material
  • Barrel and hardware materials, colors, length
  • Tags, storage location
  • Purchase source, date, and price
  • Notes (ink currently loaded, impressions, history, etc.)
📱 On a phone — tapping the photo area will let you take a photo with your camera or choose one from your gallery, making it easy to photograph a pen and add it on the spot.

Browsing your collection

Use the toolbar to search, filter, and sort your pens. Switch between grid view (photo cards) and list view (compact table) using the buttons on the right of the toolbar. Click any pen to see its full details, and use the Edit or Delete buttons from the detail view.

Importing from the Pens app

If you have an existing collection in the Pens app, you can import it directly:

  • In the Pens app, go to Settings → Export → HTML
  • Save the exported .html file to your device
  • In Pen Collection, tap ⬆ Import and select the file
  • All your pens and photos will be imported automatically

Backup and restore

Your data lives in your browser's local storage, which means it can be lost if you clear your browser data. Export a backup regularly.

  • To back up: click ⬇ Export — this downloads a .json file with all your pens and photos included.
  • To restore: click ⬆ Import and select your .json backup file. Existing pens are matched by ID so there will be no duplicates.

Moving your collection to another device

  • Export a backup on your current device
  • Transfer the .json file to the new device (email, cloud storage, cable, etc.)
  • Open the app on the new device and tap ⬆ Import
📱 Android tip — save the .json file to your Downloads folder before importing. Chrome on Android works most reliably with files in Downloads rather than cloud storage locations.

Clearing all data

The 🗑 Clear All button removes every pen from this device. This is useful when testing an import or starting fresh. Make sure you have an exported backup before using it — it cannot be undone.
